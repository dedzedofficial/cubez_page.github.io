Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to CubeZ ("CubeZ", "we", "our", or "us").

Your privacy is a core design principle of CubeZ. The application is built to operate primarily on your local device, giving you complete control over your projects, settings, and data.

By using CubeZ, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Local-First Design

CubeZ is designed so that your information remains on your own computer whenever possible.

The application does not operate a cloud platform for storing user projects, prompts, generated content, configuration files, API credentials, or personal information.

Unless you intentionally use an online integration, your data never leaves your device.

Information Stored Locally

CubeZ may store the following information on your device:

  • Application settings
  • User preferences
  • Projects
  • Prompts
  • Generated content
  • Workflow configurations
  • API credentials and access tokens that you choose to save
  • Local logs created for troubleshooting

This information is stored only on your computer and is not transmitted to CubeZ.

Third-Party Integrations

CubeZ supports optional integrations with third-party platforms, including but not limited to:

  • TikTok
  • Twitch
  • YouTube
  • Discord
  • Bluesky
  • Mastodon
  • Facebook
  • VRChat

When you choose to use one of these integrations, CubeZ sends only the information necessary to complete the action you requested.

Examples include:

  • Authenticating with an external service
  • Publishing content
  • Retrieving account information
  • Uploading media
  • Reading platform-specific data after you have granted permission

These communications occur directly between your device and the relevant third-party service.

CubeZ does not retain copies of information transmitted through these integrations.

Analytics

CubeZ does not collect analytics regarding your projects or personal usage.

If future versions introduce optional analytics, they will be disabled by default unless otherwise stated.

Cookies

The CubeZ application itself does not use cookies.

Third-party services accessed through your web browser may use their own cookies according to their respective privacy policies.

Data Security

Because your information is stored locally, you are responsible for securing your device.

We recommend:

  • Using strong operating system security
  • Encrypting your storage where available
  • Keeping regular backups
  • Protecting API credentials you choose to save

Data Retention

CubeZ does not retain your personal information because it is not stored on CubeZ-operated servers.

Deleting local files or uninstalling the application may permanently remove your stored data.

Your Rights

Because CubeZ stores your information locally, you maintain direct control over your data.

You may:

  • View your stored information
  • Modify your information
  • Export your information
  • Delete your information at any time

without requesting permission from CubeZ.

CubeZ Clicker (Android Mobile Game)

CubeZ Clicker is a separate, mobile-only product in the CubeZ family, distributed on Google Play. Because it works differently from the local-first desktop tools described above, it is covered here specifically.

CubeZ Clicker does not collect personal information directly and does not operate its own servers. Your progress (cube counts, upgrades, skins, achievements) is stored locally on your device.

Advertising. CubeZ Clicker shows ads served by Google AdMob, which may use your advertising ID and device information to show and measure ads, per Google's own privacy policy. You can opt out of personalised ads in your device's Google settings. A one-time in-app purchase permanently removes ads and is processed entirely by Google Play Billing, we never see your payment details.

Cloud save. If you sign in with Google Play Games, your save data is stored via Google Play Games Services under your Google account, governed by Google's privacy policy. You can delete this save data from Google Play Games' own settings at any time.

YouTube / Twitch checks. To notify you about new videos or live streams, the app periodically fetches public information from YouTube and Twitch about one specific channel. No personal or account data of yours is sent to either service by this feature.

Children. CubeZ Clicker is not directed at children and is not designed to collect personal information from anyone, regardless of age.

Changes to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

The latest version will always be published on the official CubeZ website.

Contact

Questions regarding this Privacy Policy may be directed to the CubeZ project administrator.
