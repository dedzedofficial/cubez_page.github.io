# CubeZ Privacy Policy **Last Updated:** July 14, 2026 ## Introduction Welcome to CubeZ ("CubeZ", "we", "our", or "us"). Your privacy is a core design principle of CubeZ. The application is built to operate primarily on your local device, giving you complete control over your projects, settings, and data. By using CubeZ, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. --- ## Local-First Design CubeZ is designed so that your information remains on your own computer whenever possible. The application does not operate a cloud platform for storing user projects, prompts, generated content, configuration files, API credentials, or personal information. Unless you intentionally use an online integration, your data never leaves your device. --- ## Information Stored Locally CubeZ may store the following information on your device: * Application settings * User preferences * Projects * Prompts * Generated content * Workflow configurations * API credentials and access tokens that you choose to save * Local logs created for troubleshooting This information is stored only on your computer and is not transmitted to CubeZ. --- ## Third-Party Integrations CubeZ supports optional integrations with third-party platforms, including but not limited to: * TikTok * Twitch * YouTube * Discord * Bluesky * Mastodon * Facebook * VRChat When you choose to use one of these integrations, CubeZ sends only the information necessary to complete the action you requested. Examples include: * Authenticating with an external service * Publishing content * Retrieving account information * Uploading media * Reading platform-specific data after you have granted permission These communications occur directly between your device and the relevant third-party service. CubeZ does not retain copies of information transmitted through these integrations. --- ## Analytics CubeZ does not collect analytics regarding your projects or personal usage. If future versions introduce optional analytics, they will be disabled by default unless otherwise stated. --- ## Cookies The CubeZ application itself does not use cookies. Third-party services accessed through your web browser may use their own cookies according to their respective privacy policies. --- ## Data Security Because your information is stored locally, you are responsible for securing your device. We recommend: * Using strong operating system security * Encrypting your storage where available * Keeping regular backups * Protecting API credentials you choose to save --- ## Data Retention CubeZ does not retain your personal information because it is not stored on CubeZ-operated servers. Deleting local files or uninstalling the application may permanently remove your stored data. --- ## Your Rights Because CubeZ stores your information locally, you maintain direct control over your data. You may: * View your stored information * Modify your information * Export your information * Delete your information at any time without requesting permission from CubeZ. --- ## Changes to this Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be published on the official CubeZ website. --- ## Contact Questions regarding this Privacy Policy may be directed to the CubeZ project administrator.
